# Billing and Payments

This document covers how billing works at Northwind Cloud, accepted payment methods, and how to manage your subscription.

## Billing cycle

- **Monthly plans**: charged on the same day of each month as your initial signup. If signup date is the 31st, billing falls on the last day of months without a 31st.
- **Annual plans**: charged once per year, paid upfront. Receives a 20% discount versus monthly.
- **Pro-rated upgrades**: upgrading mid-cycle is pro-rated to the day. Downgrades take effect at next renewal.

## Accepted payment methods

| Method | Plans |
|--------|-------|
| Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover) | All paid plans |
| ACH direct debit | US customers, all paid plans |
| SEPA direct debit | EU customers, Business and above |
| Wire transfer | Enterprise only, $5K minimum invoice |
| Purchase orders | Enterprise only, on approved credit |

We do not accept cryptocurrency, PayPal, or international money orders.

## Currency

- USD is the default and required for all plans
- EUR billing available on Business and Enterprise plans
- GBP billing available on Business and Enterprise plans
- Other currencies: contact sales for Enterprise

## Invoices and receipts

- Invoices are generated automatically and emailed to the billing contact
- PDF copies available in **Settings → Billing → Invoices**
- VAT / GST is added automatically based on billing address
- For VAT-exempt entities, upload your exemption certificate in billing settings

## Refunds and cancellations

- Cancel anytime from **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**
- Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
- No refunds for partial months on monthly plans
- Annual plans: pro-rated refund available within first 30 days only
- Enterprise contracts: refer to contract terms

## Failed payments

- Card decline: we retry on day 3, 5, and 7 after failure
- After 3 failed retries, workspace access is suspended (read-only mode)
- After 14 days of suspension, workspace data is archived
- After 90 days of archival, data is permanently deleted

To restore service, update payment method in billing settings and contact support@northwind.cloud if needed.
