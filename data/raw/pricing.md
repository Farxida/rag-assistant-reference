# Pricing

Northwind Cloud offers four pricing tiers designed for teams from individual analysts to large enterprises. All prices are in USD and billed monthly unless noted. Annual billing receives a 20% discount.

## Plans

### Free
- Up to 3 users
- 5 GB data storage
- 10K queries per month
- Community support only
- Public dashboards only

### Starter — $49/month per workspace
- Up to 10 users
- 50 GB data storage
- 100K queries per month
- Email support, 24h response SLA
- Private dashboards
- 30-day query history

### Business — $299/month per workspace
- Up to 50 users
- 500 GB data storage
- 1M queries per month
- Priority email + chat support, 4h response SLA
- SSO via SAML 2.0
- 12-month query history
- Audit logs
- Custom branding

### Enterprise — custom pricing
- Unlimited users
- Custom storage tier
- Unlimited queries
- Dedicated support engineer, 1h response SLA
- SSO + SCIM provisioning
- HIPAA / SOC 2 Type II compliance
- Single-tenant deployment option
- 99.95% uptime SLA with credits

## Add-ons

- **Additional storage**: $10/month per 100 GB above plan
- **Query overage**: $0.0005 per query above plan limit
- **Premium connectors** (Snowflake, Databricks): $99/month flat
- **Advanced AI features** (forecasting, anomaly detection): $199/month flat

## Free trial

All paid plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start. Trial usage is not metered against plan limits.

## Billing and invoices

- Monthly billing on the day of signup
- Annual billing receives a 20% discount, paid upfront
- Invoices issued via Stripe; PDF available in account settings
- Accepted payment: credit card, ACH (US only), wire transfer (Enterprise only)
- Currency: USD by default, EUR and GBP available on Business and Enterprise plans
